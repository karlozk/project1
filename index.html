What features does the HealthTracker application offer?

HealthTracker offers activity tracking, health metrics monitoring, nutrition logging, personalized insights and health reports.

How do I create an account in the HealthTracker application?

Download the app, sign up with your email, verify your address, complete your profile, and start tracking your health.

How can I track my health data?

Log in, manually input data or sync with devices, and review your progress on the dashboard.

Is my health data secure with HealthTracker?

Yes, your data is encrypted, protected by multi-factor authentication, and never shared without your consent.

Where can I find the latest health news?

Find the latest health news in the "News" section of the app, customize your feed, and enable notifications for updates.
